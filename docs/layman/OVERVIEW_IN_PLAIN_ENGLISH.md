# ShieldBase In Plain English

ShieldBase is an insurance assistant that can do two jobs in one chat:

1. answer insurance questions
2. help a user get a quote step by step

## Why this project exists

Most chatbots are good at talking but bad at completing structured workflows.

Most forms are good at collecting data but bad at handling natural conversation.

This project tries to combine both.

## What makes it useful

The user can:

- ask what products are offered
- ask what coverage means
- start a quote
- answer one question at a time
- interrupt the quote to ask another question
- continue the quote from the same place

## What makes it safer than a pure AI chatbot

The quote math is not generated by the AI model.

Instead:

- the AI helps answer knowledge questions
- the backend handles workflow steps and quote calculation with fixed rules

That means the important business logic is more controlled and easier to explain.
