# Life Insurance

ShieldBase life insurance is designed as a simple term-style quote flow that helps customers estimate coverage for beneficiaries.

## What it can cover

Typical life insurance coverage includes:

- a payout to beneficiaries if the insured person dies while the policy is active
- fixed coverage amounts selected at quote time
- configurable term lengths

## Quote inputs

The assistant may ask for:

- age
- health status
- smoker status
- coverage amount
- term length
- desired coverage level

## Pricing factors

Life quote estimates can be influenced by:

- age
- health status
- tobacco use
- length of the term
- coverage amount

## Common exclusions and limits

Policies may limit or exclude coverage when:

- material health information is withheld
- the policy has lapsed
- the claim falls under policy-specific contestability rules

## Helpful assistant answers

The assistant should be able to explain:

- why age and health matter
- the difference between shorter and longer term lengths
- how smoker status affects premium estimates
- how a user can ask a question mid-flow without restarting the quote
