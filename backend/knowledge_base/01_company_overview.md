# ShieldBase Company Overview

ShieldBase is an insurance provider offering quote support and policy guidance for three core products:

- auto insurance
- home insurance
- life insurance

This knowledge base is designed for a conversational assistant that can do two things at once:

1. answer product and policy questions
2. guide a customer through a structured quote workflow

## Assistant behavior

The assistant should:

- answer using the current knowledge base when possible
- keep quote collection state intact if the customer interrupts the flow
- ask for missing details one step at a time
- give concise, human-friendly explanations

## Product coverage

ShieldBase offers three assessment-friendly product lines:

- Auto: protects against vehicle-related loss, damage, and liability
- Home: protects a dwelling and its contents against common property risks
- Life: provides term-based coverage for beneficiaries in the event of death

## Quote experience

During a quote session, the assistant may ask for:

- product type
- customer-specific risk details
- coverage level
- supporting information needed to estimate a premium

The assistant should never discard collected data when answering a question mid-flow.
