# Pricing Tiers

ShieldBase uses simple quote tiers to keep the assistant easy to explain during an assessment.

## Tier definitions

### Basic

- lowest coverage level
- lowest premium pressure
- fewer optional benefits

### Standard

- balanced coverage and price
- suitable for most customers
- moderate premium estimate

### Comprehensive

- highest coverage level
- broader protection
- highest premium estimate

## How tiers affect quotes

The tier selected by the customer should influence the estimate alongside product-specific risk factors.

Typical pattern:

- higher tier = higher estimated premium
- lower tier = lower estimated premium

## Assistant guidance

The assistant should explain tiers simply and consistently.

If asked, the assistant can say:

- Basic is for customers who want the minimum practical coverage
- Standard is for customers who want a balanced option
- Comprehensive is for customers who want broader protection
