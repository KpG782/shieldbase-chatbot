# Auto Insurance

ShieldBase auto insurance is designed to help customers understand vehicle coverage and obtain a quote quickly.

## What it can cover

Typical auto insurance coverage includes:

- liability coverage for damage or injury you cause to others
- collision coverage for damage to your own vehicle after a crash
- comprehensive coverage for theft, vandalism, fire, weather, or other non-collision events
- uninsured or underinsured motorist coverage
- medical payments or personal injury protection, depending on the policy design
- roadside assistance and rental reimbursement as add-on options

## Quote inputs

The assistant may ask for:

- vehicle year
- vehicle make
- vehicle model
- driver age
- accident history in the last five years
- desired coverage level

## Pricing factors

Auto quote estimates can be influenced by:

- newer or more expensive vehicles
- younger drivers
- recent accidents or claims
- higher coverage tiers
- vehicle usage and garaging risk

## Common exclusions and limits

Coverage usually does not apply to:

- intentional damage
- racing or illegal vehicle use
- failure to disclose important driver information
- wear and tear or mechanical breakdown

## Helpful assistant answers

The assistant should be able to explain:

- the difference between collision and comprehensive coverage
- why driver age matters
- why vehicle year affects premium estimates
- how the quote flow works if the user changes topics mid-process
