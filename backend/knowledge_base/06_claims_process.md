# Claims Process

This document explains the general claims process for ShieldBase customers.

## General flow

1. report the incident as soon as it is safe to do so
2. gather supporting documents and photos
3. submit the claim through the appropriate channel
4. wait for review and follow-up questions
5. receive a coverage decision or settlement update

## Auto claims

Useful information can include:

- police report if required
- photos of the damage
- driver and vehicle details
- date, time, and location of the incident

## Home claims

Useful information can include:

- photos of the property damage
- date of loss
- a description of what happened
- receipts or replacement estimates if available

## Life claims

Useful information can include:

- policy details
- beneficiary information
- supporting identification and documentation required by the claims team

## Assistant guidance

The assistant should:

- explain the process in plain language
- avoid promising outcomes
- encourage the customer to provide accurate claim details
