# Home Insurance

ShieldBase home insurance is designed to help customers understand basic property protection and collect enough information for a quote.

## What it can cover

Typical home insurance coverage includes:

- dwelling protection for the structure itself
- personal property protection for furniture, electronics, and belongings
- personal liability coverage if someone is injured on the property
- additional living expenses if a covered loss makes the home temporarily uninhabitable

## Quote inputs

The assistant may ask for:

- property type
- location
- estimated property value
- year built
- desired coverage level

## Pricing factors

Home quote estimates can be influenced by:

- property value
- property age
- location risk
- type of dwelling
- chosen coverage tier

## Common exclusions and limits

Coverage usually does not apply to:

- flood damage unless separate flood coverage exists
- earthquake damage unless separate earthquake coverage exists
- maintenance neglect
- normal wear and tear

## Helpful assistant answers

The assistant should be able to explain:

- the difference between dwelling and personal property coverage
- how location affects pricing
- why year built matters
- what happens if the user asks a policy question while in the middle of a quote
