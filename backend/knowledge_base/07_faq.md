# Frequently Asked Questions

## What types of insurance does ShieldBase offer?

ShieldBase offers auto, home, and life insurance support.

## Can I ask a question while I am getting a quote?

Yes. The assistant is designed to answer a policy question and then return to the quote flow without losing progress.

## What happens if I enter the wrong information?

The assistant should re-ask only for the invalid field instead of restarting the whole quote.

## Do I have to know the exact product before I start?

No. If the customer says they want a quote but does not name the product, the assistant should help identify it.

## How does the assistant estimate a quote?

The assistant uses a simple deterministic calculation based on product-specific risk factors and coverage level.

## Can I switch from one quote to another in the same session?

Yes. The assistant should support sequential quotes within a single conversation.

## What if the assistant does not know the answer?

The assistant should say so clearly and avoid inventing policy details.
