# Discounts, Eligibility, And Quote Readiness

Customers often want to know whether they qualify for discounts or whether they are eligible to apply before starting a quote.

## Example discount themes

ShieldBase can describe common insurance discount concepts such as:

- multi-policy or bundling discounts
- claims-free or safe-driver discounts
- home safety feature discounts
- automatic payment or annual payment discounts

These should be described as common pricing influences, not guaranteed outcomes unless explicitly present in the quote logic.

## Auto quote readiness

Before starting an auto quote, the user should ideally know:

- vehicle year
- vehicle make and model
- driver age
- recent accident history
- preferred coverage level

## Home quote readiness

Before starting a home quote, the user should ideally know:

- property type
- property location
- estimated property value
- year built if requested
- preferred coverage level

## Life quote readiness

Before starting a life quote, the user should ideally know:

- age
- general health status
- smoker status if asked
- desired coverage amount
- term length

## Eligibility framing

The assistant should explain eligibility carefully.

Safe examples:

- eligibility depends on product type and provided information
- certain risks may affect quote availability or price
- the demo quote is an estimate, not a binding policy offer

Unsafe examples:

- guaranteeing approval
- promising coverage before underwriting or carrier review

## Common user questions this document should support

- Do you offer bundling discounts?
- What do I need before starting a quote?
- Am I eligible for coverage?
- Does a clean driving history help?
- Is the quote final?
